Chicken Alfredo Pasta

Description

Chicken Alfredo Pasta is a creamy and indulgent dish that combines tender pieces of chicken with a rich Alfredo sauce. The sauce, made with butter, heavy cream, and Parmesan cheese, coats the fettuccine pasta, creating a luscious and satisfying meal. The addition of garlic and Italian herbs enhances the flavor profile, making this pasta dish a favorite for those who appreciate decadent comfort food.

This recipe is perfect for a special dinner or when you want to treat yourself to a comforting and flavorful meal. The creamy Alfredo sauce pairs perfectly with the succulent chicken, creating a delightful combination that will leave your taste buds craving more. Serve it with a sprinkle of fresh parsley and a side of garlic bread for a complete and satisfying dining experience.

Ingredients

  • 8 oz (about 225g) fettuccine pasta
  • 1 lb (about 450g) boneless, skinless chicken breasts, diced
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • 1 teaspoon dried Italian herbs (optional)
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Steps

  1. Cook the fettuccine pasta according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. Season the diced chicken with salt and black pepper.
  3. In a large skillet, melt butter over medium heat. Add minced garlic and saut√© until fragrant.
  4. Add the seasoned chicken to the skillet and cook until browned and cooked through.
  5. Pour in the heavy cream, stirring continuously. Add grated Parmesan cheese and continue to stir until the sauce is smooth and creamy.
  6. Season with salt, black pepper, and dried Italian herbs (if using). Adjust the consistency by adding more cream if needed.
  7. Add the cooked fettuccine to the skillet, tossing to coat the pasta evenly with the Alfredo sauce.
  8. Serve hot, garnished with fresh parsley. Enjoy your delicious Chicken Alfredo Pasta!
