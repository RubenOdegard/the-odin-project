Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish that brings together layers of rich and flavorful ingredients. In this recipe, savory ground beef or Italian sausage is cooked to perfection and combined with a well-seasoned tomato sauce, creating a robust and hearty filling. The layers of lasagna noodles are generously spread with a creamy mixture of ricotta cheese and egg, adding a luxurious texture to each bite. Topped with a generous amount of melted mozzarella and grated Parmesan cheese, the lasagna is baked to golden perfection, creating a deliciously satisfying and comforting meal for any occasion.

This homemade lasagna recipe is a labor of love, allowing you to create a mouthwatering dish that will surely become a family favorite. The careful balance of spices and the combination of cheeses contribute to the depth of flavor, making each layer a delightful experience. Whether you're serving it for a special gathering or a cozy family dinner, this lasagna is bound to impress with its comforting aroma and irresistible taste. Take the time to savor the layers of goodness, and enjoy the warmth and comfort that a homemade lasagna brings to the table.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound (about 450g) ground beef or Italian sausage
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 ounces) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cans (6 ounces each) tomato paste
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 2 teaspoons dried basil leaves
  • 2 teaspoons dried oregano leaves
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 3 cups ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 3 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 12 lasagna noodles, cooked and drained
  • Fresh basil or parsley for garnish (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the ground beef or sausage until browned. Add onions and garlic, and cook until onions are softened.
  3. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, water, basil, oregano, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 15-20 minutes.
  4. Combine ricotta cheese and beaten egg in a bowl.
  5. In a 9x13-inch baking dish, layer meat sauce, cooked lasagna noodles, ricotta mixture, and cheeses. Repeat layers.
  6. Top the final layer with mozzarella and Parmesan cheese.
  7. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake for an additional 25-30 minutes until golden.
  8. Allow the lasagna to rest for 10 minutes before slicing.
  9. Garnish with fresh basil or parsley if desired. Serve and enjoy!
