Vegetarian Pizza

Description

Vegetarian Pizza is a delightful and colorful dish that celebrates the flavors of fresh vegetables and aromatic herbs. This pizza features a thin and crispy crust topped with a vibrant array of colorful vegetables such as bell peppers, cherry tomatoes, olives, onions, and mushrooms. The pizza is generously covered with melted mozzarella cheese and a zesty tomato sauce, creating a delicious and satisfying vegetarian meal.

This recipe allows you to customize your pizza with your favorite vegetables and herbs, making it a versatile and enjoyable dish for gatherings or casual dinners. The combination of the crispiness of the crust, the gooeyness of the cheese, and the freshness of the vegetables makes every bite a delightful experience. Enjoy this Vegetarian Pizza as a wholesome and flavorful option for your pizza nights.

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza dough (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/4 cup sliced black olives
  • 1/2 cup sliced red onions
  • 1/2 cup sliced mushrooms
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to the temperature recommended for your pizza dough.
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
  3. Transfer the rolled-out dough to a pizza stone or baking sheet.
  4. Spread an even layer of tomato sauce over the pizza dough.
  5. Sprinkle shredded mozzarella cheese evenly over the sauce.
  6. Arrange the sliced bell pepper, cherry tomatoes, olives, red onions, and mushrooms on top of the cheese.
  7. Drizzle olive oil over the vegetables and season with salt and black pepper.
  8. Bake in the preheated oven according to the pizza dough instructions or until the crust is golden and the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  9. Remove from the oven, garnish with fresh basil leaves, slice, and enjoy your delicious Vegetarian Pizza!
